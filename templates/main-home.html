Welcome to ClubHub: a platform designed for all school activities and events.

What is ClubHub?

For larger schools with thousands of students, keeping track of paperwork and information related to the hundreds of clubs on campus is no simple task. ClubHub aims to solve this problem by creating a place where students can easily access relevant, updated information about clubs. Instead of having to register during a fixed time in a scenario where contact information is often lost, users have the option to sign-up anytime, anyplace. Creating an account allows them to register for a variety of clubs. As a platform combining previously separated areas of online school resources, ClubHub will revolutionize the way schools record student participation in all related events.

Functions

ClubHub allows students to view and join the various clubs offered by specific schools. Administrators like teachers and staff can create new clubs instantly, simplifying the registration process. Club officers constantly update the information displayed. Based on the hierarchy of users, ranging from super administrators to interested students, ClubHub grants different permissions and rights. At the sign up page, users are prompted to create an account storing all of their personal information related to the clubs. In addition, students have the option to be added to the mailing list for clubs.
